2023-2024
A great way for first time members to start becoming active in VSA is through coming to our GBM's. GBM's stand for General Board Meetings. These take place bi-weekly at 6:00pm and are open to any TCNJ student to come join. Activities range from tasting delicious Vietnamese Spring Rolls or competitive man-hunt tournaments. We look forward to seeing you there!

Past Events
VSA BY NIGHT
VSA's largest event to date. A event influenced by the popular show Paris By Night a Vietnamese American television program. VSA members and TCNJ students and staff enjoyed free Vietnamese food from Fried Spring Rolls (Chả Giò), Spring Rolls (Gỏi Cuốn), Viet Sausages (Chả Lụa). The program also consisted of live performances and a open dance floor with a DJ!
